AI Legal Assistance of Pakistan


Problem Statement

Navigating through legal matters in Pakistan can feel overwhelming, especially when it comes to understanding complex legal terms and processes. Many people face difficulties in figuring out how to deal with common issues like property disputes, inheritance problems, or family law concerns. This lack of clarity often leads to frustration and delays in resolving issues. Moreover, the cost of legal advice can sometimes make it harder for people to seek help.

Introduction

We are introducing a chatbot that is designed to make legal information easy to understand. Our goal is to simplify complicated legal language and break down complex concepts so that anyone can easily grasp their rights and options. This chatbot not only helps people understand legal issues but also promotes legal awareness, encouraging everyone to know their rights. By providing straightforward advice, we aim to empower individuals to approach their legal challenges with confidence, knowing that legal knowledge should be accessible to all, regardless of background or education.
